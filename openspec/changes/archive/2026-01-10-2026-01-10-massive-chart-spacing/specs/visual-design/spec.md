# Spec: Massive Chart Spacing

## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Zen Visual Style
The application MUST use extreme whitespace to achieve a minimalist, airy aesthetic.

#### Scenario: Extreme Spacing
- **Given** the Relationship Chart is displayed
- **Then** the padding around the chart content MUST be at least 10rem (160px) on desktop.
- **And** the vertical gap between the top and bottom Bazi groups MUST be at least 15rem (240px).
